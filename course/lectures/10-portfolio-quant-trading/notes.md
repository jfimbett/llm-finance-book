# Lecture 10: Portfolio Optimization and Quantitative Trading

## Learning Objectives

After this lecture, students will be able to:
- Explain the foundations of classical portfolio theory and identify where LLMs can and cannot add value
- Use LLMs to extract alternative data signals and construct factors for quantitative models
- Design and evaluate LLM-guided algorithmic trading strategies
- Apply rigorous backtesting methodology to LLM-based signals
- Assess risk management techniques that leverage textual information
