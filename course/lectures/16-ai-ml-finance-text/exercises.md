## Exercise 3 [A] — LLMs vs. Earlier NLP in Finance

Compare bag-of-words sentiment analysis (e.g., using the Loughran–McDonald
word list) with a transformer-based LLM for the task of classifying the
sentiment of an earnings call transcript.

(a) Describe the key technical differences between the two approaches.  
(b) Design a small empirical study (max 200 words) that would let you decide
    which approach performs better for a specific downstream task (e.g.,
    predicting next-day stock return).  
(c) What limitations does each approach have in the finance context?

*Relates to Section 5: From Bag-of-Words to Large Language Models.*
