Meridian Robotics Inc. — Form 10-K (fiscal year 2025) — Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (excerpt)

Results of operations. Revenue increased 22% to 2.40 billion dollars in fiscal 2025 from 1.97
billion dollars in fiscal 2024. Growth was led by the collaborative-robot segment, where unit
shipments rose 34% as automotive and electronics customers expanded factory automation.

Gross margin. Consolidated gross margin was 58% in fiscal 2025, up from 55% in fiscal 2024, as a
larger share of revenue came from higher-margin software and service contracts attached to our
installed base of robots.

Research and development. Research and development expense was 360 million dollars in fiscal 2025,
or roughly 15% of revenue, reflecting continued investment in autonomy software and a new
warehouse-vehicle platform expected to ship next year.

Liquidity. We ended fiscal 2025 with 540 million dollars in cash and equivalents and 150 million
dollars of total debt. Free cash flow was 280 million dollars. We believe existing cash and
operating cash flow are sufficient to fund operations for at least the next twelve months.
