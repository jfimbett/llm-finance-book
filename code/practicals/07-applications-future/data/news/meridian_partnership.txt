Meridian Robotics — News wire (fictional) — Partnership announcement

Meridian Robotics announced a multi-year partnership with a major European logistics operator to
deploy its new autonomous warehouse vehicles across twelve distribution centers. Under the
agreement, Meridian will supply the vehicles and its fleet-orchestration software, and the
logistics operator will share anonymized operating data to help train Meridian's autonomy models.

Management said the partnership is expected to begin contributing to revenue in the second half of
next fiscal year and validates the company's expansion beyond fixed factory robots into mobile
warehouse autonomy. No financial terms were disclosed.
