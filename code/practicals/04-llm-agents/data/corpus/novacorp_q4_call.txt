NovaCorp Inc. — Q4 FY2024 Earnings Call (excerpt)

CEO remarks. Total revenue for the fourth quarter was 312 million dollars, up 18% year over
year, driven by strong adoption of our cloud analytics platform. Full-year revenue reached
1.14 billion dollars. We added 240 net new enterprise customers in the quarter.

CFO remarks. Gross margin expanded to 64%, up from 61% a year ago, as higher-margin software
revenue grew faster than hardware. Operating cash flow was 88 million dollars for the quarter.
We are raising full-year guidance for next year to a revenue range of 1.32 to 1.38 billion
dollars.

Analyst Q&A. On customer concentration, the CFO noted that the top three customers fell from
51% of revenue last year to 48% this year as the enterprise base broadened. On currency, the
CFO reiterated that about 40% of revenue is in euros and pounds and that a stronger dollar was
a two-point headwind to reported growth in the quarter.
