# Lecture 7: Other Applications in Finance and Future Trends

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students will be able to:

1. Survey the breadth of LLM applications in finance beyond valuation and credit risk, including portfolio management, regulatory compliance, and risk reporting.
2. Design and implement automated financial workflows using LLM-based pipelines, triggers, and orchestrators.
3. Build a retrieval-augmented research assistant for financial documents, and describe the OpenClaw framework for financial research agents.
4. Identify sources of bias in LLM-based financial systems and apply fairness metrics and mitigation strategies.
5. Explain the regulatory constraints on LLM deployment in finance (SR 11-7, MiFID II, GDPR) and implement model risk management practices.
6. Articulate the key future trends in LLM finance research and identify open problems.
