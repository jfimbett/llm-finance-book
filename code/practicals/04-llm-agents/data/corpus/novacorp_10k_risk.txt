NovaCorp Inc. — Form 10-K (fiscal year 2024) — Item 1A. Risk Factors (excerpt)

Concentration of revenue. A substantial portion of our revenue is derived from our three
largest customers, which together accounted for approximately 48% of total revenue in fiscal
2024. The loss of any one of these customers, or a material reduction in their orders, would
have a material adverse effect on our results of operations.

Supply chain. We depend on a limited number of contract manufacturers located in Southeast
Asia. Disruptions from natural disasters, tariffs, or geopolitical tension could delay
shipments and increase component costs. We carry approximately 70 days of inventory to
mitigate short-term disruption, but prolonged interruptions could impair our ability to meet
demand.

Foreign currency. Roughly 40% of our revenue is denominated in euros and British pounds.
A strengthening U.S. dollar reduces reported revenue and can compress gross margin, which we
only partially hedge with forward contracts.

Regulation. Our cloud analytics products process personal data and are subject to the EU
General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar regimes. Non-compliance could result in
fines of up to 4% of global annual revenue.
