\chapter{Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Text in Finance}
\label{ch:ai-ml-finance-text}

\begin{chapterintro}
This chapter provides the conceptual foundation for the rest of the book.
We define artificial intelligence, trace its relationship to machine learning
and deep learning, motivate its application to finance, and explain why the
prevalence of text in financial markets makes large language models an
especially powerful tool for practitioners and researchers alike.
\end{chapterintro}
