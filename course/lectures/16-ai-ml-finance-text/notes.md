# Lecture 16: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Text in Finance

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, students should be able to:

1. Define artificial intelligence and distinguish it from machine learning and deep learning.
2. Describe the AI–ML–deep learning hierarchy and place large language models within it.
3. Articulate three concrete ways AI creates value in financial markets and institutions.
4. Identify the main sources of unstructured text in finance and explain why text is challenging to process.
5. Trace the evolution of NLP approaches from bag-of-words models to transformer-based LLMs.
6. Explain why the textual richness of finance makes LLMs a particularly powerful tool in this domain.
