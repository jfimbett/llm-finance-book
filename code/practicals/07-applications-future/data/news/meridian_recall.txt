Meridian Robotics — News wire (fictional) — Product recall

Meridian Robotics issued a voluntary recall of one model of its collaborative robot arm after
reports that a firmware bug could cause the arm to stop unexpectedly during operation. The company
said no injuries were reported and that the issue affects roughly 1,200 units shipped in the prior
two quarters.

Meridian will push a free firmware update to fix the defect and said it does not expect a material
financial impact. Analysts noted the recall is a reminder of the safety and liability risks the
company faces as it moves into autonomous warehouse vehicles.
