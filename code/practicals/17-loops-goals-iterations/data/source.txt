Meridian Robotics Inc. — Fiscal 2025 Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year Results (fictional)

Revenue. Full-year revenue rose 22% to 2.4 billion dollars, up from 1.97 billion dollars in
fiscal 2024. Growth was led by the autonomous-warehouse division, where unit shipments more
than doubled and service-contract revenue grew 41%.

Profitability. Operating margin expanded to 17% in fiscal 2025 from 13% a year earlier, as
higher-margin software attach rates and manufacturing scale outpaced a 6% increase in
operating expenses. Net income was 286 million dollars.

Orders. Order backlog reached 3.1 billion dollars at year end, a record, reflecting three new
multi-year fleet agreements signed in the fourth quarter.

Cash flow. The company generated free cash flow of 310 million dollars and ended the year with
540 million dollars in cash and no debt drawn on its 250 million dollar revolving facility.

Outlook. Management issued guidance for fiscal 2026 calling for revenue growth of 15% to 18%
and operating margin of roughly 18%, assuming no material change in component costs.
