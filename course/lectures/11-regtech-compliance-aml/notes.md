# Lecture 11: RegTech, Compliance, and Anti-Money Laundering

## Learning Objectives

After this lecture, students will be able to:
- Describe the regulatory landscape for AI in finance, including the EU AI Act, MiFID II, Basel III, and GDPR
- Apply LLM-based and agentic techniques to anti-money laundering and adverse media screening
- Design KYC and sanctions screening pipelines using entity resolution and PEP identification
- Automate regulatory reporting tasks such as XBRL tagging and SAR drafting
- Evaluate governance frameworks and model risk management requirements for LLM-based compliance systems
