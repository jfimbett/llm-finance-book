NovaCorp Inc. — Form 10-K (fiscal year 2024) — Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis (excerpt)

Results of operations. Revenue increased 18% to 1.14 billion dollars in fiscal 2024 from 966
million dollars in fiscal 2023. The increase was primarily attributable to growth in the cloud
analytics segment, where subscription revenue grew 31%. Hardware revenue was approximately flat.

Gross margin. Consolidated gross margin was 64% in fiscal 2024 compared to 61% in fiscal 2023.
The improvement reflects a favorable shift in mix toward software subscriptions.

Liquidity. As of the end of fiscal 2024 we held 420 million dollars in cash and cash
equivalents and had no outstanding borrowings under our 200 million dollar revolving credit
facility. We believe existing cash and operating cash flow are sufficient to fund operations
and planned capital expenditures for at least the next twelve months.

Capital expenditures. Capital expenditures were 54 million dollars in fiscal 2024, primarily
for data-center capacity to support the growth of the cloud analytics platform.
