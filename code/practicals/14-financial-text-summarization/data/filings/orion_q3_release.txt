Orion Dynamics Inc. — Q3 FY2025 Earnings Release (excerpt)

Orion Dynamics Inc. (NASDAQ: ORDY) today reported financial results for the third
quarter of fiscal 2025.

Revenue. Total revenue was $1.46 billion in the third quarter of fiscal 2025, an
increase of 22% from $1.20 billion in the same quarter a year ago, driven by demand for
the company's autonomous logistics platform.

Profitability. Gross margin was 58% in the third quarter, up from 54% a year ago,
reflecting a favorable shift toward higher-margin software subscriptions. Diluted
earnings per share were $1.27, compared with $0.91 in the prior-year quarter.

Guidance. For full fiscal year 2025, Orion Dynamics raised its revenue guidance to a
range of $5.90 billion to $6.10 billion, and now expects full-year diluted EPS of $4.80
to $5.00.

Cash flow. Operating cash flow was $214 million for the quarter, and the company ended
the quarter with $730 million in cash and cash equivalents.
